Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions our customers ask.

❓

What services does Rok Your TV offer?

We offer a range of connectivity and streaming solutions including internet, TV bundles, and on-demand content — all with affordable pricing and fast setup.

🌐

Which providers are available?

We work with top-tier providers including AT&T, Verizon, Xfinity, HughesNet, and more. Our team helps you choose the best fit for your location and budget.

🏢

Is Rok Your TV a provider?

No, we’re an authorized dealer that connects you with leading service providers and manages your installation and support process.

💸

Are there setup or activation charges?

Yes, our service execution charges range from ₹499 to ₹1499 depending on plan type and provider. These cover connection, support, and account management.

🧾

Why separate billing from the provider?

Since we manage service execution independently, our charges are billed separately from your provider's regular internet or TV charges.

📞

How can I contact support?

You can contact us through the website contact form or call our 24/7 support line for troubleshooting, plan changes, or new service setup.
